How it works

Three steps.
That's genuinely it.

No training days. No tech setup. No new hardware. If your staff can read a WhatsApp message, they can use ScanFlow.

Step 01
QR code on every table
We print a unique QR code for each table in your restaurant. Laminate it, stick it on — done forever. Each code links directly to your menu for that table.
Step 02
Customer scans & orders
Your full menu opens on their phone — photos, prices, veg/non-veg dots, categories. They tap items, add to cart, and place the order. No app download. No login.
Step 03
Staff sees it instantly
The order appears on your staff's phone the moment it's placed. Table number, every item, total. Mark it Preparing — then Done.
