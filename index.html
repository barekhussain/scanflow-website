How it works

We set you up once. You run it from day one.

Your first visit is done by us, in person. After that, the owner dashboard is yours — no waiting on us to change a price.

01📋

We onboard you, in person

We visit your restaurant, load your entire menu, print QR codes for every table, and create your owner login plus a login for each waiter. Takes about one hour.

02🛠️

You're in control from here

Add dishes, change prices, mark items out of stock, add or remove a waiter's login — all from your own dashboard, anytime you like. No call needed.

03🔔

Customers order, staff serve

Customer scans the table QR and orders from their phone. It lands on the right waiter's tab instantly — table, items, total. Mark it Preparing, Ready, then Served.
